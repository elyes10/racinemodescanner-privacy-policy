Privacy Policy – Racine Mode Production Scanner

Last updated: 24/11/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Racine Mode (“we”, “our”, “the Company”) handles information within the Racine Mode Production Scanner mobile application (“the App”). The App is an internal enterprise tool used exclusively by authorized company employees for production tracking inside the textile factory. No external users are allowed to create accounts or access the system.

1. Purpose of the App

The App is designed to support internal production operations by:

  • Scanning packets and recording production steps
  • Synchronizing data with the company’s ERP system
  • Allowing supervisors to monitor workflow progress

This App is not intended for use by the general public.

2. Information We Collect

The App may process the following internal operational data:

2.1 User Account Information

  • Employee ID
  • Username assigned by the company
  • No public account creation is possible

All accounts are created and managed internally by the Company’s IT department.

2.2 Production & Operational Data

  • Packet barcodes
  • Scan timestamps
  • Workstation progress
  • Assigned operators

This data is strictly related to internal manufacturing processes.

3. How We Use the Information

Data collected by the App is used exclusively for:

  • Updating the ERP system
  • Tracking production status
  • Improving workflow accuracy
  • Ensuring quality and traceability of textile manufacturing

No personal data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored on company-controlled servers and databases. No data is stored or transmitted to third parties.

5. Permissions Used

The App may request:

  • Camera access (for barcode scanning)

No other device permissions are required.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Google Play Services
  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Any external SDKs

The App communicates only with Racine Mode’s internal ERP systems.

7. Data Security

We apply organizational and technical measures to protect internal company data, including:

  • User authentication
  • Secure communication channels
  • Access restrictions

Only authorized employees may use the App.

8. User Rights

Since all accounts belong to employees and are controlled internally, users may request:

  • Correction of incorrect internal data
  • Deletion of accounts when no longer employed

Such requests must be submitted to the company IT department.

9. Contact Information

For any privacy-related concerns, please contact:

Racine Mode – IT Department
Email: contact@racinemode.com

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Updates will be published on this page.
