Privacy Policy – Racine Mode Scanner

Developer: Elyes Zarrad

Last updated: 24/11/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Elyes Zarrad (“we”, “our”, “the Developer”) handles information within the Racine Mode Scanner mobile application (“the App”). The App is an internal enterprise tool used exclusively by authorized company employees for production tracking inside the textile factory. No external users are allowed to create accounts or access the system.

1. Purpose of the App

The App is designed to support internal production operations by:

  • Scanning packets and recording production steps
  • Synchronizing data with the company’s ERP system
  • Allowing supervisors to monitor workflow progress

This App is not intended for public use.

2. Information We Collect

The App may process the following internal operational data:

2.1 User Account Information

  • Employee ID
  • Username assigned by the company

All accounts are created and managed internally by the company’s IT department. No public account creation is possible.

2.2 Production & Operational Data

  • Packet barcodes
  • Scan timestamps
  • Workstation progress
  • Assigned operators

3. How We Use the Information

Data collected by the App is used exclusively for:

  • Updating the ERP system
  • Tracking production status
  • Improving workflow accuracy
  • Ensuring manufacturing traceability

No information is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored on company-controlled servers and never transmitted to third parties.

5. Permissions Used

The App may request:

  • Camera access (for barcode scanning)

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Google Play Services
  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Any external tracking tools

7. Data Security

We apply technical and organizational measures including:

  • Secure authentication
  • Restricted internal access
  • Protected communication channels

8. User Rights

Employees may request:

  • Correction of inaccurate internal data
  • Account removal when employment ends
